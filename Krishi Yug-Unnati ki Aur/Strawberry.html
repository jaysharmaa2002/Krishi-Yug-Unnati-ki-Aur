Strawberry

Strawberry- Strawberries are one of the easiest fruit to grow and great for beginners! Plus, they are very rewarding because the taste is far more flavorful than what you'll ever find in a grocery store. Why The sugar in berries converts to starch soon after they're picked. Learn more about growing strawberries in the home garden. Complete information about Strawberry cultivation and organic farming Strawberry is a very delicate fruit. Which can be grown in different types of soil and climate. Its plant can produce fruit in a few months. Which is mildly sour and mildly sweet in taste. It is heart shaped in appearance. And its color is bright red.
The best thing about strawberries is that they're very easy to grow in almost all climates and soils across the United States and Canada-as long as you plant them in a location that gets full sun.Strawberry fruits are very tempting, juicy and nutritious. Strawberry is one of the favorite fruits all over the world for its color, appearance and taste. Which is a special crop grown in cold regions. Which is now being done in Bihar, Punjab and Madhya Pradesh of the country.


Strawberry varieties You will be surprised to know that 600 varieties of strawberries are present in this world. All of them differ from each other in their taste and colour. Strawberry is known for its unique aroma. Many varieties of strawberries are grown. But mainly the following varieties are produced in Haryana.
Camaroza - This is a variety developed in California and is a short-term fruiting variety. Its fruit is very big and strong. The smell of this fruit is good. This variety gives fruit for a long time and is virus resistant.
Oso Grand - This is also a variety developed in California. Which gives fruit in short days. Its fruit is large and is good for eating and making products. But the problem of bursting can be seen in its fruit. This variety can produce a large number of runners.
Ofra -This variety has been developed in Israel. It is an early variety and its fruit production starts early.
Chandler - This is a variety developed in California. It can be produced under various conditions. Its fruit is attractive. But its skin is delicate.
Sweet Charlie -Plants of this variety give early fruit. Its fruit is sweet. The plant has resistance to many fungal diseases.

Planting time Planting should be done from September 10 to October 10. Planting can be started after some time i.e. till 20th September in case of high temperature at the time of transplanting.

Planting Strawberries Its plants are planted in raised beds. The width of these beds is 105-110 cm. And the height is about 25 cm. is kept. 55 cm between two beds. difference is kept. In the beds, the plants should be placed 25 cm between the four rows. The distance between the plant and the plant is 25-30 cm. It is necessary to keep Planting should be done in the cooler time of the day.

Soil selection for strawberries For getting good yield, sandy loam soil is considered suitable. Soil with pH 5.0 to 6.5 is also suitable for its cultivation.

Climate This crop is a temperate climate crop for which 20 to 30 degree temperature is suitable. When the temperature rises, plant damage occurs and yield is affected.

Farm Preparation In the first week of September, plow the field thrice and then spread 75 tonnes of well-rotted manure in one hectare of land and mix it in the soil. Along with, potash and phosphorus should also be added while preparing the field on the basis of soil test.

Food and Fertilizer Strawberry plant is very delicate. Therefore it is necessary to give him food and fertilizers from time to time. Which is given after seeing the soil test report of your farm. After mulching, give liquid fertilizer through drip irrigation. In which nitrogen phosphorus p2o5 and potash k2o should be given from time to time after taking advice of agricultural scientists.

Strawberry pests and diseases Insects like moths, flies chafer, strawberry root weevils, strawberry mantis, juice beetles, strawberry nectar mites can harm it. For this, put neem oil in the roots of plants, apart from this, leaf spot on the leaves, powdery mildew, can be affected by leaf blight. For this, after identifying plant diseases from time to time, spray insecticides on the advice of scientists.

Low Tunnel Access To protect the plants from frost, it is necessary to cover the raised beds with a transparent sheet of polythene whose thickness is 100-200 microns. Bamboo sticks or hoops made of iron wire should be used to keep the sheet above the beds. Do the cover work before sunset and after sunrise remove it from the polythene sheet.

Irrigation Irrigation should be done immediately after planting, irrigation should be done from time to time keeping in mind the moisture, before fruiting in strawberries, irrigation can be done by micro fountain, after fruiting, irrigation should be done by drip method only.

Strawberry plucking When the color of the fruit becomes 70 percent original, then it should be plucked. If the market is at a distance, it should break a little harder. Harvesting should be done on different days. Strawberry fruit should not be caught. The punishment should be held from above. Average fruit yield is 7 to 12 tonnes per hectare.

Packing Strawberries should be packed in plastic plates. It should be kept in a ventilated place. Where the temperature is five degrees. After a day the temperature should be zero degree.

Grant from the Government There is also a grant from the Horticulture and Agriculture Department in different states. In which 40 to 50% subsidy is also available on plastic mulching and drip irrigation, sprinkler irrigation etc.
