ABOUT US

Technology can answer most difficulties farmers face. But, farmers have lack of information. Technology can assist them with proper information. Indian farmers can adopt technology to assist them at every step as efficiently as farmers in developed countries.

It is envisaged to make available relevant information and services to the farming community and private sector through the use of information and communication technologies.

कृषि युग is an agricultural website which facilitates farmers to get information about seeds, seasonal crops, fertilizers, pesticides etc. The chatbot in our website listens to user query and give them answer likewise.

We have created a portal for farmers not only to sell their produce at a better rate but also to help them by guiding in such a way that both the sides, i.e., farmers and buyers all over India will benefit.

This responsive site will provide many functionalities like chatbot, tracking facility, speech to text conversion, etc to make the website accessible, attractive and user-friendly.

We are aiming to get the required facilities, support and help to the users which might help them avoid certain undesirable circumstances and unforeseen loss.
