About Us

"Krishi Yug - Unnati Ki Aur"


"Agriculture is the backbone of the Indian Economy" -said Mahatma Gandhi six decades ago. Even today, the situation is still the same, with almost the entire economy being sustained by agriculture, which is the mainstay of the villages. It contributes 16% of the overall GDP and accounts for employment of approximately 52% of the Indian population. Rapid growth in agriculture is essential not only for self-reliance but also to earn valuable foreign exchange.

The biggest trouble is that the farmers don't get the price they deserve for their produce. This is basically a result of numerous intermediaries. They don't have information about better quality pesticides to protect their crops from bugs, diseases and weed.Technology can answer most difficulties farmers face. But, farmers have lack of information. Technology can assist them with proper information. Indian farmers can adopt technology to assist them at every step as efficiently as farmers in developed countries.

It is envisaged to make available relevant information and services to the farming community and private sector through the use of information and communication technologies. Our portal, Krishi Yug is an agricultural website which facilitates farmers to get information about seeds, seasonal crops, fertilizers, pesticides etc. The chatbot in our website listens to user query and give them answer likewise.

We have created a portal for farmers not only to sell their produce at a better rate but also to help them by guiding in such a way that both the sides, i.e., farmers and buyers all over India will benefit. This responsive site will provide many functionalities like chatbot, tracking facility, speech to text conversion, etc to make the website accessible, attractive and user-friendly. We are aiming to get the required facilities, support and help to the users which might help them avoid certain undesirable circumstances and unforeseen loss.
