Kaitha

Kaitha- You know, the botanical name of Kaith, (Kapitth) (Kabit) is Limonia acidissima and in English it is known as Wood Apple or Monkey Fruit. Kaith trees are deciduous and are also found in large numbers in forests. Kaith trees are abundant in the hilly areas of North India.But their numbers are decreasing in populated areas. In southern Asia and Java, these trees are grown in gardens around homes. Plants grown from seed are capable of producing fruit in about 15 years. The oil extracted from cath leaves has been used medicinally for centuries to treat a variety of ailments, including the treatment of scabies. The taste of ripe cath pulp is sour-sweet and its seeds are attached to the pulp itself.It is called wood apple, curd fruit, elephant apple or monkey fruit in English and as Feronia elephantum or Feronia limonia in the scientific dictum.


Time of sowing The time of propagation by the seeds of Kaith is suitable for high summer and early days of monsoon (May-June month).

Distance Generally the distance of pits for plantation should be 8 meters.

Sowing method For planting cath's seedlings, nursery is prepared by seeds and plants are planted by grafting method.

Planting Method For planting saplings, pits of 3x3x3 feet should be dug in the field in the month of April-May and the pits should be left open so that they get good sunlight and the pits should be free from underground insects. And at the time of planting, the pits should be filled with 3-4 baskets of rotted cow dung, 20-25 kg. Mixing sand and 1 kg of lime should be filled up to a height of 6-8 inches.

Seed treatment The seeds are immersed in water for about 12 hours after which they are sown directly in the field or in the nursery.

Favorable climate Kaith is a plant of sub-tropical climate. It is an altitude of 4000 feet above sea level where the temperature rises to 49 degree centigrade in summer and falls to -7 degree centigrade in winter, where Kaitha tree can grow.

Land selection The cath tree can be grown in many types of soil, it can also be easily grown in swampy, alkaline, stony soil. For this the pH of the soil. The value should be 5-8.

Farm preparation For the cultivation of cath, clean the land thoroughly to make it weed free. Plow his field well. After that the pits should be dug.

Fertilizers and chemical Fertilizers For good production in the cultivation of cath, one year old plant should be given 10 kg. In cow dung manure or vermicompost and chemical fertilizers, 500 grams of nitrogen, 250 grams of phosphorus, 500 grams of potash should be applied per tree per year. Manure and fertilizers should be applied in full quantity in the month of June-July.

Weed control To control weeds, weeding should be done as needed.

Irrigation One or two years of irrigation is required for good growth of new plants. Established plants can live well without irrigation.

Fruit Picking When the color of the fruits changes from dark green to yellowish green, then harvest the fruit by 2 cm. Should be done with the stalk. While plucking, care should be taken that the fruits should not fall on the ground. Due to this the skin of the fruit gets cracked, due to which the fruit rots from inside.

Storage The fruits of cath should be stored in a dry place free from moisture.
